Parties & platforms

Quick overview of major parties so you can compare and decide.

Left / Progressive

Social Democrats & Greens

Focus on climate, welfare, and public services. Typical themes: green transition, healthcare, education.

Centre

Liberals & Centrists

Market-friendly with social safety nets. Often stress economy, EU integration, and civil rights.

Right / Conservative

Conservatives & Right

Emphasis on tradition, law and order, lower taxes, and national identity. Often stress security and borders.

Check official party websites and voter guides in your country for exact names and programmes.

What you learn when you engage with politics and voting.

  • 1

    Critical thinking

    Weigh policies, spot misinformation, and form your own views from evidence.

  • 2

    Civic literacy

    Understand how elections, parliaments, and governments actually work.

  • 3

    Informed debate

    Discuss issues clearly and respectfully with people who disagree.

  • 4

    Accountability

    Hold representatives to their promises and use your vote to demand change.
